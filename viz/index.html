Bird migration flow visualization

This visualization shows a one week bird migration event in April 2013 recorded by 5 weather radars, by interpolating the data retrieved from the radars (shown in blue) over the whole area. This proof of concept is the result of a 5 day hackathon by the LifeWatch INBO team. The long-term goal of the European Network for the Radar Surveillance of Animal Movement (ENRAM) is to retrieve this kind of data at a European scale. Documentation and source code are available on GitHub.
